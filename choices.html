Impact on future life

The research and developments of neuroprosthetics to use electrodes to interface with the nervous system will significantly improve bionic body parts. This new input and output method will simulate the same seamless movements of a natural body part. This will ultimately enhance the mobility, and the quality of life for the individual as the prosthesis will seem as lifelike as if they had never lost a limb.

The second point I would like to make is related to the first one and has to do with the safety of bionic technology. There are many people who are concerned about the safety of bionic technology and whether or not it is safe for humans to use. The third point I would like to make is about the long-term effects of bionic technology. There is not a lot of research on the long-term effects of bionic technology, so we don't really know what could happen to a person if they use this technology for an extended period of time. There are some people who are concerned that the long-term effects of bionic technology could be harmful to a person's health.
